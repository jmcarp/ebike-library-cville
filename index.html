Read about us in Charlottesville Tomorrow!

We’re a free e-bike lending library serving the Charlottesville area. You can visit and and test-ride any of our e-bikes, or take one home for up to a week at a time. You can sign up to borrow an e-bike using our sign-up form, or by emailing us at hi@ebikelibrarycville.org.
