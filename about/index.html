About

E-bikes are a great way to replace short car trips. But most affordable e-bikes are sold online, so it’s not always easy to try an e-bike before you buy one. The Charlottesville E-bike Lending Library is a free service that lends out e-bikes for up to a week so you can try an e-bike (or a few) and decide whether you want to get one yourself. Sign up to borrow an e-bike or contact us with any questions.

Our E-bikes

  • RadRunner Plus: Utility e-bike

  • Ride1Up Prodigy: Commuter e-bike

  • RadMission: Single-speed e-bike

  • RadWagon 4: Cargo e-bike (temporarily unavailable)
